About Me

My name is Miranda Chang. I was born and raised in Pittsburgh and am passionate about the Pittsburgh food scene. I have been a vegetarian for 13 years, so understand that my recommendations are based on vegetarian food picks. I am currently an MBA candidate at the Kellogg School of Management but am living in Pittsburgh again while classes are virtual. I am excited to share my favorite tastes of Pittsburgh with you and hope that you will support these local gems during this challenging time for small businesses.

My intent is to continue trying new spots around Pittsburgh and adding content to the site. If you have any suggestions, especially those with tasty vegetarian options, I welcome your suggestions on the Contact page. I hope you find the recommendations and content helpful; I welcome input on the pieces of restaurant information that you would find most valuable as well!

Thanks, and enjoy the eats!
