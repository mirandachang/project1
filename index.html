About Me

My name is Miranda Chang. I was born and raised in Pittsburgh and am passionate about the Pittsburgh food scene. I am currently an MBA candidate at the Kellogg School of Management but am living in Pittsburgh again because we have all virtual classes this quarter due to the restrictions from Covid-19. I am excited to share my favorite tastes of Pittsburgh with you and hope that you will support these local gems during this challenging time for small businesses.
